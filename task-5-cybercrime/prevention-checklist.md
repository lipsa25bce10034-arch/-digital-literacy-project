# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone
- Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown persons
- Always verify payment requests before approving them
- Use official banking and UPI apps only
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts
- Check transaction messages carefully
- Do not click on suspicious links or messages
- Report cyber fraud at https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline 1930 immediately in case of financial fraud
