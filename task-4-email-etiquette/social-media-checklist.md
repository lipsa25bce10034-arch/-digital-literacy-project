# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for Students

## ✅ Do's
- Maintain a professional and respectful profile
- Share useful and informative content
- Use privacy settings to protect personal data
- Verify information before sharing it
- Think before posting anything online

## ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Avoid engaging in online arguments or conflicts
- Do not spread false or misleading information
